Operating procedure

One word.
Seven minutes of paranoia.

Each round runs the same protocol. The only variable is who's lying — and how well.

PHASE 01

Assemble

The host opens a room and gets a 4-digit Game ID. Agents join with a callsign. No accounts, no installs — share the code, you're in.

PHASE 02

The Word

One player becomes the Word Master and sets a secret word. The role rotates every round, so everyone runs the room eventually.

PHASE 03

Eyes Only

Each player taps the eye to privately see the word — except one, who sees YOU ARE THE MOLE. 20s AUTO-SEAL

PHASE 04

Interrogation

Phones down, eyes up. Talk it out face to face. Vague answers feel suspicious. Specific answers give the word away. Walk the line.

PHASE 05

Debrief

The Mole is unmasked. Celebrate, accuse, demand a rematch — then a new Word Master takes over. The operation runs players × 2 rounds.
